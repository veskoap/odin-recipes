Pesto Pasta

Description

This is a delicious, vegetarian pasta recipe packed with the flavor of fresh basil and walnut pesto.

Homemade Pesto Sauce is the best use for all the fresh summer basil growing in your herb garden.

Follow my tried and true tips to make a perfect pesto pasta that will not turn black or bitter.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Basil
  • Walnuts
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta: Boil water in a saucepan with salt. Add the desired shape of pasta and cook till al-dente. Reserve 1 cup of boiled pasta water. Drain the remaining water. Transfer the cooked pasta to a mixing bowl.
  2. Make Pesto Sauce: Start with blending the ingredients in a food processor to make the pesto sauce.
  3. Combine: Add pesto sauce to the cooked pasta. Mix nicely, add ¼ cup of reserved pasta water to make it saucy. Add the remaining ¼ cup of water, cream, cherry tomato, mix, and the basil pesto pasta is ready to serve.
